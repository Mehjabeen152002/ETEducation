About Our Department

The Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering at RUET is one of the pioneering departments in Bangladesh, offering comprehensive education in electronics, telecommunications, and related fields.

Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for successful careers in the rapidly evolving technology sector.

The department maintains strong relationships with industry partners, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for professional challenges and opportunities in the field of electronics and telecommunications.

We are committed to fostering innovation, research, and academic excellence while maintaining high standards of education that meet international criteria.
