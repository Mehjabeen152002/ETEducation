Facilities

Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Rental Library

The Department of ETE has a well-equipped rental library. The library has a collection of application-specific textbooks, reference books on various fields of electronics and telecommunication engineering. It also has a huge collection of undergraduate and post-graduate thesis reports. The library is accessible for the students from 8 AM to 5 PM on all workdays.

Classrooms

This department has four well-decorated classrooms. Each classroom has the capacity for seating sixty students. There is also a very big hall room which can be used for conducting examinations. Moreover, a well-decorated modern seminar room is available for hosting seminars and presentations.

Laboratories

The department offers state-of-the-art laboratories, including electronics, communication, and digital signal processing labs for practical learning. Below are some of the specialized laboratories:

  • Electronics Lab
  • Communication Lab
  • Digital Signal Processing Lab
  • Antenna and Propagation Lab
  • Computer Lab
